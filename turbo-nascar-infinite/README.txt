🦅 TURBO NASCAR INFINITE 🦅

HOW TO PLAY
  1. Double-click index.html (opens in your browser — no internet needed)
  2. Press ENTER

DRIVE
  Arrow keys — ↑ gas · ↓ brake · ← → steer

SURVIVE
  Don't let THE PACK behind you catch you.
  Don't hit the wall too fast.
  Every lap = a new round = everything gets worse.

DIE (you will)
  Deaths earn Freedom Points.
  Spend them in the GARAGE on upgrades and weapons:
  Q rockets · W guns · E mines (stalls the pack) · R eagle · T turbo

REPEAT
  Get further every run. Reach round 10 and you're officially
  better than the robot that balance-tested this game.

M = mute · ESC = pause
Progress saves automatically in your browser.
